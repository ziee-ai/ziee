- **DRIFT-1.1** ‚Äî verdict: impl-wins ‚Äî PLAN.md ITEM-1 specified `w-auto` on the trigger to make it content-sized. **That is wrong and was reverted.** A `<button>` is a form control, so `width: auto` is SHRINK-TO-FIT, not fill-available: the trigger sized itself to its text and simply ignored the space on offer, overflowing its container (measured: a 320px trigger inside a 274px composer at a 390px viewport, with the label spilling past the composer edge). The kit's own `w-full` is what gives the required behavior ‚Äî the slot wrapper is a flex item whose base size is the label's max-content (content-sized when there is room) and which shrinks under pressure. The final className drops the width override entirely and keeps only `min-w-0 max-w-[20rem]`. Caught by TEST-4, which is why it asserts wide-vs-narrow rather than a single state. PLAN.md's ITEM-1 text is superseded by this entry; the observable behavior it specified (full name when there is room) is unchanged and is what TEST-2 asserts.
- **DRIFT-1.2** ‚Äî verdict: impl-wins ‚Äî a bound the plan did not have: `max-w-[60%]` on the composer's right toolbar group. PLAN.md assumed flex shrink alone would make the model name yield. It does not protect the LEFT group, which is `flex-1` with a ZERO basis and therefore never competes for space ‚Äî it receives only what the right group leaves over. Measured at a 390px viewport: the right group took its full 364px and the left group was left 2px, so its `shrink-0` "+" button overflowed into the selector. Capping the right group against the toolbar ROW (a definite width ‚Äî no container query needed, so DEC-3's rejection of `@container` still holds) guarantees the left actions keep ~40%. TEST-4 now asserts the "+" button never overlaps the trigger, in both regimes.
- **DRIFT-1.3** ‚Äî verdict: impl-wins ‚Äî TESTS.md placed TEST-6 / TEST-7 (sidebar caption alignment) in `tests/e2e/visual/` as gallery specs. The gallery CANNOT render them: its `PageFrame` mounts a route's element WITHOUT its layout (`sdk/packages/gallery/src/runtime/pages.tsx:126-175`), so the real `LeftSidebar` ‚Äî and the module slots that populate Navigation / Tools / the Recent-chats widget ‚Äî never appear there. (Corroborated by `GEOMETRY_FINDINGS.jsonl` containing no `layout-sidebar-*` selector at all, and by `coverage.ts` marking `LeftSidebar` as `kind: 'via'` on a page that does not in fact render it.) Both specs moved to `tests/e2e/layouts/sidebar-title-alignment.spec.ts`, a full-stack e2e against the real app shell, alongside the existing `layouts/sidebar-toggle.spec.ts`. Same tier (`e2e`), same TEST-IDs, and strictly MORE faithful ‚Äî B7 wants the real thing run.

Coverage is strictly larger than planned (it now also proves the ceiling bounds a pathological name), and the two seeds share one `holdSingleModel` helper.
